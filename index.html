OUR SIGNATURE CLASSIC COFFEE & FRESHLY BAKED CHOCO CHIPS COOKIES

The unmistakable full-flavour of Classic Coffee is what makes our signature coffee so loved all over the world. Savour the wonderfully rich and refreshing aroma of this medium-dark roast. Melted butter, more brown sugar than white sugar, cornstarch, and an extra egg yolk guarantee the absolute chewiest chocolate chip cookie texture. And you don’t even need a mixer! This Best Ever Chocolate Chip Cookie recipe has the magic ratio of brown to white sugar. While this seems like a small detail it makes a huge difference in the texture of the cookie. The Best Ever Chocolate Chip Cookie dough is chilled before baking in order to give the dough a better texture and to meld the flavors.
